Our Story

Crafted with Love, Delivered with Care

Aura Boxed Gifts is a custom gift hamper business that focuses on creating beautifully curated, personalized gift boxes for different occasions.

💝 What we do:
• Create customized hamper boxes (you can choose items, theme, colors)
• Design aesthetic packaging with lights, shredded paper, ribbons, etc.
• Offer occasion-based gifting like Birthdays, Mother's Day, Weddings, Anniversaries, and Corporate gifts.

We provide pan-India delivery and take orders via our website or Instagram DMs! 🎁
